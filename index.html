The Figure 8 Group LLC

The Figure 8 Group LLC focuses on perfecting the execution of your entire operation. Understanding what “Figure 8” represents, if you are an owner or a manager and you touch tables, go to the bar, oversee the expo window, spend time at the host stand... Knowing your restaurant every shift will lead you to a profitable P&L.

“If you don’t take care of your guests, another restaurant will.”
